
# Carbon Footprint for Organization (CFO) Research Paper

## Abstract
This paper discusses the importance of measuring Carbon Footprint for Organization (CFO) in industrial sectors. CFO is a method to evaluate the total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions caused directly and indirectly by an organization.

## Key Findings
1. Scope 1: Direct emissions from owned or controlled sources.
2. Scope 2: Indirect emissions from the generation of purchased energy.
3. Scope 3: All other indirect emissions that occur in a company’s value chain.

## Methodology
The study follows the ISO 14064-1 standard and the GHG Protocol.

## Conclusion
Organizations that implement CFO reporting can better manage their environmental impact and comply with international regulations.
